These are my hobbies:

I like cycling, hiking, travel, clay sculptures, and crochet.

I learned cycling when I was a child, and since then, only the size of the bikes has changed. It is a passion.

I believe the best way to know a place is by hiking. Whenever possible, I enjoy spending a lot of time walking around the cities and sometimes I even take some pictures.

I have visited around 15 countries so far but would like to travel even more. Traveling feels like imagining how others live in that place. In my opinion, it is important to know not only the tourist routes but the real-life in each place.

Now I'm learning knitting in a volunteer program.
